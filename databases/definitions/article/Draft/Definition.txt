General Sense: An article is a piece of writing included with others in a newspaper, magazine, or other publication. It is typically a written work published in a print or electronic medium. An example is a news article on a recent event or a research article in an academic journal.

Business Context: In a business context, an article can refer to a piece of writing that provides insight, advice, or information relevant to the business or industry. An example is a market analysis article in a business magazine or a blog post on a company's website about their latest product.

Programming Context: In programming, "article" might refer to a piece of writing or documentation that provides information or instruction about a particular programming concept, language, or tool. An example is an article on a developer's blog explaining how to use a new JavaScript framework, or a tutorial article on a coding website teaching how to solve a common problem in Python.
