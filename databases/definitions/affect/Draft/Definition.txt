In a general sense, "affect" is a verb that means to have an impact on or make a difference to. For example, "The weather can affect your mood."

In a business context, "affect" can refer to how decisions, changes, or events impact a company's operations, profits, or strategy. For example, "The new government regulations will affect our business operations."

In a programming context, "affect" can refer to how changes in one part of the code can impact or alter the functionality of another part. For example, "Changing the value of this variable will affect the output of the function."
