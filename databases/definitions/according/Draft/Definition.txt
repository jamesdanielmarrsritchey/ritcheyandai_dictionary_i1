In a general sense, "according" means in agreement with or as stated by. For example, "According to the weather forecast, it will rain tomorrow."

In a business context, "according" can be used to refer to actions or decisions made based on certain rules, policies, or information. For example, "According to our company policy, employees are entitled to two weeks of vacation per year."

In a programming context, "according" is not a specific term or keyword, but it can be used in a similar way to describe actions or behaviors that are in line with certain rules or conditions. For example, "According to the if-else condition, the function will return different values."
