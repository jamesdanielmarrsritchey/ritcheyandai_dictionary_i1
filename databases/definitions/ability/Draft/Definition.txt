General Definition: Ability refers to the capacity of an individual or system to perform a specific task or function. For example, a person's ability to speak multiple languages.

Business Context: In business, ability often refers to the skills or competencies that an individual or organization possesses to perform specific tasks or achieve objectives. For example, a company's ability to innovate and adapt to market changes.

Programming Context: In programming, ability can refer to the functionality that a piece of software, a system, or a programmer has. For example, a programmer's ability to write efficient algorithms, or a software's ability to handle large amounts of data.
