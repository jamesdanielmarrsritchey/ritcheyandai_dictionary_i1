In a general sense, an "artist" is a person who creates art. This could be visual art, music, literature, performance, or any other medium. For example, a painter or a musician.

In a business context, an "artist" could refer to a professional who creates products or services that are considered artistic or creative. This could include graphic designers, advertising creatives, or even chefs. For example, a graphic designer creating a logo for a company.

In a programming context, an "artist" could refer to a programmer who is particularly skilled at writing elegant, efficient, and beautiful code. This could also refer to a UI/UX designer who creates aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly interfaces. For example, a programmer who writes clean and efficient code that solves complex problems.
